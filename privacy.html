Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. Here's how we handle your information.

What Information We Collect

When you place an order or reach out to us, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Any information you provide about the person or story you're preserving
  • Payment information (processed securely by Stripe)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for one reason only: to fulfill your order.

That means:

  • We research and write your heritage book, pet tag, or business profile based on what you tell us
  • We host your legacy permanently online
  • We send you updates about your order status

We Don't Sell Your Data

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with anyone. Your story is yours. Your data stays with us, used only to keep your legacy alive.

How We Protect Your Information

Payment information is processed securely through Stripe. Personal data is stored safely and accessible only to our team who need it to fulfill your order.

Questions?
