Our Story

Most families lose their history.

Not because they don't care. Because no one built a system to hold it. Papers get thrown out. Grandparents die before they're recorded.

We started My Legacy Continues in Santa Maria because we almost lost ours. The Vazquez family line goes back to 1547 in Galicia, Spain — a confirmed hidalgo line from the Reconquista.

Every product we build is built to make sure that doesn't happen. Physical. Digital. Permanent. No subscription required.
