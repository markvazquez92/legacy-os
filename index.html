I Almost Lost Mine

My family came from Galicia, Spain. They settled in South Texas, then moved to California. Along the way, pieces got lost. Photos faded. Names were forgotten. Recipes disappeared. The stories my grandmother told were almost gone forever.

I started My Legacy Continues because I realized that if I didn't capture what remained, nobody would. And I knew I wasn't the only one. In Santa Maria, I see families working hard every day — concrete workers, ranchers, service workers — building their legacies with their hands. They deserve a simple, honest way to preserve what matters.

The stories. The traditions. The migrations. Almost gone forever.

— Bear Vazquez, Founder
