Questions
Things People Ask Us
How long does it take to receive my Heritage Book?
Most Heritage Books are ready within 4–6 weeks from the time your order is confirmed. We'll stay in touch throughout every step of the process so you're never wondering where things stand.
What if I don't know much about my family's history?
That's exactly why we exist. You give us names, a few places, and time periods — we do the deep work. Our research reaches into public records, historical archives, and census data to uncover the chapters you never knew were written.
Can I share my own photographs?
Absolutely — and we love it when families do. After your order is confirmed, we'll send instructions for sharing your photos securely. They'll be woven into your book with care.
What does the QR Legacy Code actually do?
Each book comes with a unique QR code linked to your family's permanent digital archive — viewable on any device, anytime, by anyone you choose to share it with. It keeps your story alive beyond the printed page.
Is my family's story kept private?
Completely. Your family's history and personal information are never shared, sold, or used for anything other than creating your Heritage Book. Your story belongs to you.
How does the Safety Registry Tag help rescue animals?
Every tag purchase includes a contribution to our Rescue Network, which funds transport, care, and placement for animals at our partner shelters in Santa Barbara County. When you protect your pet, you're also helping one that hasn't found a home yet.
How many copies of my Heritage Book do I receive?
Your order includes one premium hardcover book. Additional copies can be ordered at a discounted rate — perfect for gifting to family members who share the story.
