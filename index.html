I Almost Lost Mine

My grandfather Joe — a metalworker, a ring maker, a storyteller — passed away. And almost everything went with him.

The Vázquez line goes back to 1547 in Galicia, Spain. My mom's side — García and Montemayor — came through South Texas. Generations of migration, survival, building something real on the Central Coast.

But nobody had written it down. No one had recorded it. And suddenly I realized — it could be gone in one generation.

So I built this for my family. Then I built it for yours.

Heritage isn't just blood. It's stories. The names nobody writes down. Until it's gone.
