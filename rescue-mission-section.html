Our Mission

No dog should lose their story because no one wrote it down.

My Legacy Continues builds free permanent profiles for every rescue dog in our network. Not a listing that expires. Not a shelter bio that gets deleted. A real page with their name, their story, their personality, and their journey — from intake to forever home.

When a dog gets adopted, their profile stays live. It becomes their permanent record — updated with their new family, their new name if they get one, and a QR tag on their collar that brings them home if they ever get lost.

A portion of every pet tag sale goes directly to our rescue partners. No middleman. No platform fees. We build the tools. They save the dogs.
