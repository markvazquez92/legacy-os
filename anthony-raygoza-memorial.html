His Story

Who He Was

Anthony Raygoza Sr. was a father, a brother, and a fixture in the lives of the people who knew him. He was part of the extended Garcia and Raygoza family that built their lives on California's Central Coast — the kind of family that shows up for each other, that marks time by who's at the table.

He was Debbie Garcia's brother, which made him Bear Vázquez's uncle. He was the father of Antoinette Raygoza. That's not a small thing. The connections he had — the relationships he kept — those run all the way through this family's story.

This page was built so his name stays on the internet. Not as a news article. Not as a record. As a memorial — permanent, no subscription, no expiration. He was real. This page makes sure the people who search for him can find that out.
