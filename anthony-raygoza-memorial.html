In Loving Memory

Anthony Raygoza

Santa Maria, California
His Story

Anthony Raygoza was a man rooted in family, community, and the Central Coast of California. He carried himself with quiet strength — the kind of person whose presence filled a room without demanding attention.


A father, a friend, a familiar face in Santa Maria. His story deserved to be told and kept — not left to memory alone. This profile is his permanent record.

Words to Remember

"Some people live in a way that leaves a mark on everyone they meet. Anthony was one of those people."
