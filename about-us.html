About My Legacy Continues

Preserving family heritage, honoring legacies, building connections across generations.

Our Mission

My Legacy Continues is dedicated to preserving your family's story. Whether it's documenting your heritage, creating memorial plaques, or building a professional profile that tells your story for generations to come, we help you leave a lasting legacy.

Founded by Mark "Bear" Vazquez in Santa Maria, California, we believe every family's history matters and deserves to be preserved, celebrated, and passed down.

What We Do

We operate across three complementary divisions:

Heritage Preservation

Heritage Culture Books — Professionally documented family histories, genealogy research, and cultural preservation in a luxury hardcover format ($199)

Memorials & Legacy

Cemetery Plaques ($129) and Pet Legacy Tags ($24.99-$49.99) — Honor those you've lost with lasting memorials

Professional Profiles

Business & Heritage Profiles ($79) — Professional documentation of your story, business, or family legacy online

Baby Legacy

Baby Legacy Packages ($79) — Create a documented legacy story for a new life, capturing the family's hopes and history
