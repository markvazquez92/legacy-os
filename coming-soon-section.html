Building in Public

What We're Building Next

Most rescues run on spreadsheets, group texts, and hope. We're building the system they should have had years ago — and giving it to them for free.

Phase 2

Transport Coordination

Dogs get pulled from shelters in one city and need to reach fosters or adopters in another. Right now that's organized through DMs and Facebook groups. We're building a relay system — volunteers claim legs of the route, each handoff is confirmed, and the dog's location updates in real time on their profile. No dog gets lost in transit.

Phase 2

Foster Family Dashboard

Foster families get their own login. Track feeding schedules, vet visits, behavior notes, and medication — all visible to the rescue in real time. No more texting updates back and forth. The rescue sees everything in one place.

Phase 2

Adoption Follow-Up System

Automated check-ins at 2 weeks, 1 month, and 3 months after adoption. New owners submit photos and updates through a simple form. The rescue stays connected to every dog they placed. The dog's profile keeps growing with their new family.

Phase 3

Vet Record Vault

Every dog's full medical history stored permanently. Vaccines, surgeries, medications, allergies, heartworm status. When the dog gets adopted, the records transfer automatically. No more lost paperwork. No more starting over at a new vet.

Phase 3

Public Journey Tracker

Every dog gets a public timeline. Intake photos, foster updates, transport milestones, adoption day. Supporters and donors follow the journey and see exactly where their money went. It turns one adoption into a story that inspires the next one.

Phase 3

Disaster Response Protocol

When fires, floods, and disasters hit, animals lose everything too. We're building a rapid-deploy system — instant profiles for displaced animals, emergency transport coordination, and a reunification registry so lost pets find their families faster.

Phase 4

Multi-Rescue Network

Underdog Heroes is our first partner. The platform is built to scale. More rescues join the registry — each with their own section, their own dogs, their own dashboard. One platform. Every rescue connected. Every dog accounted for.

Phase 4

Sponsor a Dog

Can't adopt but want to help? Sponsor a specific dog's medical care, transport, or training. Your name goes on their profile as a guardian. You get updates as they progress. Direct impact, full transparency.

Every tool is free for rescue partners. Always will be. Built by My Legacy Continues.
