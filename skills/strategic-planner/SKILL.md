## Monthly Business Review

Pull Stripe data for the full month. Include:
- Total revenue vs. $5,000 goal
- Product breakdown with units sold
- Best-performing product (revenue + units)
- Average order value
- Notable patterns (day of week, referral source if available)
- Revenue trend vs. prior month

Then assess:
- What's working (double down)
- What's not moving (needs attention or cut)
- What's coming up (seasonal, planned campaigns)
- 3 specific actions for next month

## Goal Tracking & Accountability

When Bear checks in on goals:
1. Pull current Stripe revenue for the month
2. Calculate gap to $5,000 target
3. Calculate how many of each product are needed to close the gap:
   - Contractor Bundles needed: [gap ÷ 650]
   - Legacy Bundles needed: [gap ÷ 199]
   - Heritage Books needed: [gap ÷ 150]
4. Recommend the most realistic path to hit the number

Be direct. If the month is behind, say it. Tell Bear exactly what he needs to do this week to get back on track.

1. **Direct outreach to contractors** — Concrete pumpers, electricians, plumbers in Santa Maria / Santa Barbara area. Offer Contractor Bundle. High close rate because Bear speaks their language.

2. **Hispanic funeral homes (B2B)** — Cemetery Plaque + memorial profile as add-on service. Target independent Hispanic-owned funeral homes in CA, TX, NM. Wholesale deal: they upsell, MLC gets a cut.

5. **SEO + profile network** — Every profile page creates a permanent indexed page. Over time, searches for "[family name] heritage" or "[contractor name] Santa Maria" will find MLC.
