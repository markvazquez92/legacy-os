The Mission Behind the Trade

I started Bears Concrete Pumping to do more than just build foundations. I built it to fund a rescue mission. For over a decade, I’ve worked the Texas oilfields and Central Coast job sites. I use my hands to build infrastructure, and I use the profits to move dogs out of high-kill shelters.

Professional Services

  • Concrete Logistics: Specialized line pumping, slab pours, and precision footings.
  • Structural Builds: Custom framing, drywall, and complete multi-discipline remodeling.
  • Mechanical Trades: Electrical and plumbing installation for custom residential projects.
