01

You tell us the story.

Fill out our intake form or sit down with us directly. Photos, names, memories — whatever you have. If you don't have much, we help you find what's missing.

Photos, stories, timelines, connections to other people and places in the ecosystem. Then we tie it to a physical QR code on a real object — a plaque, a tag, a truck decal, a business card.

No login. No app. No subscription. Scan the QR on a headstone, a truck, a collar, or a business card and the full story opens right there on your phone. That profile lives as long as we do.
