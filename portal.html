⚜️ Heritage Highlights
🛡️
Hidalgo Status — Confirmed
The Vazquez line holds documented Hidalgo nobility status, earned through military service in the Reconquista. Coat of arms recorded in Spanish heraldic registries.
Source: Spanish heraldic registries · c. 1547, Soba, Santander
⚔️
Knights of Seville — Montemayor Line
The Montemayor line carries Hidalgo status from the Siege of Seville, 1247–1248, under King Ferdinand III. One of Spain's oldest knightly honors.
Source: Reconquista battle records · General Terán, Nuevo León
🧓
Faustino Montemayor — 98 Years
Born January 15, 1852 in Mexico. Died December 7, 1950 in Sinton, TX — age 98. Witnessed Mexican independence, Emperor Maximilian, WW2, and the atomic age.
Source: Sinton Mexican Cemetery records · Sinton, TX
🎵
Musical Heritage — Los Alegres de Terán
The Montemayor family originates from Cadereyta Jiménez, Nuevo León — 130 miles from General Terán, home of Los Alegres de Terán. This music is your cultural soundtrack.
Source: Nuevo León regional research · María Montemayor Morin, b. 1893
