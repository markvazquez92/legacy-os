Her Story

In Her Own Words — Those Who Knew Her Best

A Life Built on Love

Antoinette Raygoza lived the way she loved — fully, quietly, and without condition. Born in Santa Maria, California on October 14, 1942, she was raised in a family where faith, hard work, and togetherness were not values you talked about. They were simply how you lived.

"She never needed the spotlight. She was the light."

Antoinette spent her life as the steady center of her family. She raised her children with patience, cooked for anyone who walked through the door, and had a way of making every person feel like they were the most important one in the room. That gift was not something she practiced. It was something she was.

She found her greatest joy in the ordinary moments — Sunday dinners, grandchildren on her lap, the sound of her household full of voices. Those who were closest to her say she understood something most people spend a lifetime chasing: that a life well-lived is measured in presence, not accomplishment.

Antoinette passed on March 2, 2019, surrounded by the people she loved. She was 76 years old. She leaves behind a legacy that cannot be contained in any plaque or page — only in the people she shaped.
