Why Permanent Profiles Matter

Most rescue listings disappear the moment a dog gets adopted. The story is gone. The journey erased. We built this registry so that never happens. Every dog that comes through Underdog Heroes gets a permanent digital profile — free — so their story lives on long after adoption.
