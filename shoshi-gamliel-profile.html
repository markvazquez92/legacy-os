Real responsible rescue is slower, harder, less glamorous. It's about giving dogs a real chance to thrive forever.

The Rescue Leader

Shoshi Gamliel built Underdog Heroes from her Riverside home — her garage houses dogs that no other rescue will take. Pit bulls, bully breeds, mastiffs, rotties, cane corsos.

The ones the system wrote off. She said yes when everyone else said no.

With 45,000 Instagram followers and a track record that includes dogs featured in Newsweek and Yahoo News, Shoshi isn't just running a rescue — she's exposing a broken system and building accountability where there was none.

My Legacy Continues is building the infrastructure her mission has always needed.

What Underdog Heroes Offers

Rescue Intake

Got a dog that needs help? Submit an intake form. We'll evaluate and determine the best path forward.

Foster Coordination

Provide a temporary safe home for dogs. Every foster frees up space for another rescue.

Adoption Support

Complete adoption process with training, support, and lifetime follow-up.

Behavioral Assessment

Professional assessment of each dog's temperament, triggers, and training needs.

Transport Network

Real-time volunteer driver coordination to move dogs between rescues and adoptive homes.
