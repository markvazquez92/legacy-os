Shoshi Gamliel

Founder & President, Underdog Heroes Inc.

Underdog Heroes was founded in 2020 on a simple conviction: the dogs the system writes off deserve a real chance. From a home base in Riverside, California, Shoshi has built one of the most vocal and visible rescues in Southern California — taking in the pit bulls, bully breeds, mastiffs, cane corsos, and rotties every other rescue passes on.

Her work has been featured in Newsweek and Yahoo News, and a community of more than 45,000 supporters follows every pull, every foster, every adoption. Real responsible rescue is slower, harder, less glamorous — and it is how she has spent the last five years.

“The harsh reality is we may never break the cycle of cruelty, but we can raise awareness and make an impact.”

The Work

Rescue. Rehabilitate. Rehome.

Three steps. Every dog. Every time.

Rescue

Dogs pulled from high-kill shelters — the ones no other rescue will touch, the ones with a day left on the list.

Rehabilitate

Medical care, behavior work, patient training, and the time it takes for a dog to trust a human again.

Rehome

Matched with vetted adopters for life — safe forever homes, lifetime follow-up, no dog left behind.
