Her Life

Who She Was

Antoinette Raygoza was family in the truest sense of the word — not just by blood, but by presence. She was one of those people who made a room feel fuller. Her laugh was real. Her care was real. The kind of person who remembered your birthday and actually showed up.

She was part of the Central Coast fabric — the extended Vazquez and Raygoza family that built their lives in Santa Barbara and Santa Maria over generations. Her story is part of that larger story.

This page was built so her name stays on the internet. Permanent. No subscription. No expiration. She was real, and this page makes sure people can find that out long after we're all gone.
