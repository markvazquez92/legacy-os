Her Story

Her Life

Deborah Mahony was born August 30, 1969 — the daughter of John Garcia and Elvira Garcia. She is part of a family line that runs deep through the Central Coast of California.

She is the mother of three: Mark, Joseph, and Stephanie Vazquez.

Her mother, Elvira Garcia, was born September 7, 1944 — the youngest of eighteen children born to Clara and Francisco Garcia in Sinton, Texas.

The Garcia and Montemayor lines are documented back to the 1940 Census — a family that built roots across Texas and California across generations.

Deborah's story is part of a larger legacy — one that connects the Vazquez family, the Garcia line, and everything being preserved here at My Legacy Continues.

This profile lives permanently so her children and grandchildren always have a place to come back to.
