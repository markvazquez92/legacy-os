## BUSINESS MODEL & DIVISIONS

**Price:** $199 per book  
**Target Customer:** Families wanting to preserve their history, family reunions, genealogy researchers

- Selling Price: $199

- Gross Margin: $154 (77%)

- Hourly Rate: ~$13-19 per hour (low, but upfront volume play)

**Volume Targets:**
- Month 1: 2-3 books = $400-600 revenue
- Month 3: 5-8 books = $1,000-1,600 revenue
- Year 1: 20-30 books = $4,000-6,000 revenue

**Customer Acquisition:**
- Word-of-mouth (your family connections, local networks)

## RISK ANALYSIS & MITIGATION

### Risk 1: Low Initial Sales
**Risk:** Network doesn't convert to customers in month 1  
**Mitigation:** 
- Start with concrete customers (captive audience)
- Offer first-customer discount (20% off) to get testimonials
- Pre-sell 2-3 heritage books in month 0 before launch

### Risk 2: Printing/Production Delays
**Risk:** Vendor delays cause customer disappointment  
**Mitigation:**
- Choose vendors with 3-5 day turnaround (Blurb, CustomInk, not China)
- Buffer delivery timeline (quote 2 weeks, deliver in 1)
- Have backup vendors lined up

### Risk 3: Stripe Account Holds
**Risk:** Stripe freezes funds due to high chargeback rate or refunds  
**Mitigation:**
- Communicate clearly before purchase (what to expect)
- Have easy refund/return policy (encourages customer confidence)
- Monitor chargeback rate (keep <0.5%)

### Risk 4: Competition from Free Alternatives
**Risk:** Ancestry/FamilySearch/Canva offer DIY heritage docs  
**Mitigation:**
- Position as premium/professional (not DIY)
- Emphasize personal service + accuracy + physical product
- Focus on busy professionals who value time savings

### Risk 5: Limited Time Available
**Risk:** Concrete business takes priority, heritage services neglected  
**Mitigation:**
- Set concrete business as separate P&L with own hours
- Hire / partner for heritage research by month 4
- Automate: email templates, intake forms, design templates
