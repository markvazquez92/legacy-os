About This Company

Concrete Pumping Done Right

Bears Concrete Pumping is a family-owned concrete pumping operation based in Santa Maria, California. We pump concrete for residential foundations, commercial slabs, custom pools, hillside builds, and everything in between across Santa Barbara and San Luis Obispo County.

Owner-operated. Mark Vazquez runs every job personally or has direct oversight on every pump. When you call Bears, you are talking to the guy who shows up. We do not hand work off to crews you have never met.

We operate trailer pumps and line pumps capable of reaching tight residential lots, backyard pours, elevated decks, and multi-story commercial pours. If the concrete needs to get somewhere difficult, that is exactly where we specialize.

Tight-Access & Hillside
