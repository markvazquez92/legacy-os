Why This Exists

Most families lose the early years before they even realize it.

A phone dies. A hard drive crashes. Photos get lost in a move. The first five years disappear faster than any other.

My Legacy Continues was built for working families who want something permanent — not a cloud app that charges you monthly, not a social media page that deletes your account. One page. One payment. Lives there forever.

This is also the foundation for a Heritage Book later. When your child grows up and wants to know where they came from — the profile is already there, ready to become something bigger.
