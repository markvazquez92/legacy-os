Bear's Story

My grandfather Joe was a metalworker. A ring maker. A storyteller.
When he passed, almost everything went with him.
The Vázquez line goes back to 1547 in Galicia, Spain.
My mom's side — García and Montemayor — came through South Texas.
Generations of migration. Survival. Building something real on the Central Coast.
Nobody wrote it down. So I built this for my family. Then I built it for yours.

— Bear Vázquez, Founder, Santa Maria CA
