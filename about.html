Bear's Story

My grandfather Joe was a metalworker. A ring maker. A storyteller.

When he passed, almost everything went with him.

The Vázquez line goes back to 1547 in Galicia, Spain. My mom's side — García and Montemayor — came through South Texas.

But nobody had written it down. And suddenly I realized: it could be gone in one generation.

So I built this for my family. Then I built it for yours.
