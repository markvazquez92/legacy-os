Why I Built This

My Vazquez family line traces back to 1547 in Galicia, Spain. When my grandfather passed, I realized nobody had written it down. Almost gone forever.

The Founder

Mark "Bear" Vazquez

Location: Santa Maria, CA — The 805

Business: Bears Concrete Pumping + My Legacy Continues

I run a concrete pumping company by day. I built this by night — because I almost lost my family's history. My Garcia and Montemayor roots run through South Texas. My Vazquez line runs through Spain and Mexico City. My grandfather Joe was a metalworker and ring maker. When he passed, the stories almost went with him. So I built the system I wish existed. No subscriptions. No corporations owning your family's story. Just permanence.

The Model

Nipsey Hussle said own the block. Don't sell through someone else's platform. Build direct. Build permanent. Build for the community.

My Legacy Continues starts in Santa Maria. Heritage books, memorial plaques, pet tags, rescue partnerships — every product stacks into one permanent ecosystem for your family. Physical and digital. Connected forever.
