Heritage Culture Guide — Bear Vázquez

Know Where You Come From

Four weeks. Two family lines. Music, film, food, and archive.
This is your heritage — made real.

Bear carries two lines. On his father's side: the Vázquez family — Galicia, Spain through Mexico City into California. On his mother's side: the Garcia / Montemayor line — South Texas, Central Coast, eighteen children and nearly a century of American life. This guide covers both. Start with whichever side calls to you.
