Frequently Asked Questions

How does the QR code work?

Each tag has a unique QR code laser-etched onto it. When scanned with any smartphone camera, it opens your pet's permanent digital profile. The profile includes their name, photo gallery, medical info, emergency contacts, and their story. No app required.

What happens if my dog goes missing?

Anyone who finds your pet can scan the QR tag immediately. They'll see your contact information, your pet's medical needs, and emergency contacts. This is faster and more complete than a standard ID tag. For Premium tags, they'll also see behavioral notes so the finder knows how to handle your pet safely.

Can I update the profile later?

Yes. Your pet's profile is permanently hosted and can be updated anytime. Add new photos, update medical records, change emergency contacts. Premium tier includes unlimited updates forever. Basic tier includes core profile updates.

How long does the tag last?

The physical tag is made from weatherproof stainless steel or premium aluminum. It's built to last years on a collar, grave marker, or anywhere outdoors. The digital profile is hosted permanently at mylegacycontinues.com with no expiration or subscription fees.
