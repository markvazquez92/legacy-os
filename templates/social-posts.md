## Platform-Specific Notes

**Facebook:**
- Posts 1, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 10, 11, 12 perform best
- Post timing: 10am–2pm weekdays, 6pm–9pm weekends
- Use 1-2 hashtags per post (Facebook algorithm deprioritizes hashtag-heavy posts)
- Link to product pages drives traffic; consider retargeting on Facebook Ads

**Instagram:**
- Posts 2, 5, 6, 9 as feed posts; others as Stories or Reels
- Use full hashtag set (10-15) for reach
- Post timing: 11am–1pm and 7pm–9pm
- Carousel posts (#5) get 3x engagement
- Stories (24-hour) perfect for #9 (product teasers)

**LinkedIn:**
- Posts 3, 10, 11 are gold for LinkedIn audience (B2B, contractors, entrepreneurs)
- Post timing: 8am–10am weekdays
- Add 1-2 relevant hashtags + connection request to similar business pages
- Video posts (if you create demo videos) would outperform image posts 2x

**Twitter/X:**
- Shorter versions of posts (#4, #8, #11 work best)
- Use 1-2 hashtags, focus on conversational tone
- Links get better engagement on Twitter than Facebook

## Compliance & Tone Reminders

✅ All copy is authentic, warm, ADHD-friendly  
✅ All links are production-ready (mylegacycontinues.com)  
✅ All CTAs are clear and benefit-focused  
✅ No "salesy" language; focus on emotion and impact  
✅ Emojis used sparingly and only when they add clarity  
✅ All posts mention rescue partnership at least quarterly  

**Forbidden phrases:**
- "Limited time offer!" (unless ACTUAL deadline)
- "Buy now before you regret it" (pressure-y)
- "Don't miss out!" (FOMO overuse)
- "Act now!" (too salesy)

**Encouraged phrases:**
- "While you still can" (urgent, not pushy)
- "Your story matters" (emotional anchor)
- "10% supports rescue dogs" (mission tie-in)
- "Join 500+ families" (social proof without pressure)
