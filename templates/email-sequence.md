# IMPLEMENTATION NOTES

**For Mailchimp:**
1. Copy the subject line exactly (including emojis)
2. Paste body content into the editor
3. Set send dates: Day 0, 2, 4, 7, 10 from signup
4. Use merge tags: `[First Name]` = `*|FNAME|*` in Mailchimp
5. All links are live and production-ready
6. Test on mobile before sending (all emails are mobile-optimized)

**Tone Guide:**
- Short paragraphs (never more than 3 sentences per paragraph)
- **Bold** the key takeaway in each section
- Personal, warm, authentic voice (from Bear)
- Story-focused, not feature-focused
- Always include a CTA (link to product or section)
- Emphasize urgency without pressure ("while you still can" not "buy now or regret it")

**Performance Targets:**
- Email 1 (Welcome): 25-30% open rate, 5-8% click rate
- Email 2 (Rosa story): 30-35% open rate, 8-12% click rate  
- Email 3 (Product selector): 28-32% open rate, 10-15% click rate
- Email 4 (Upsell): 20-25% open rate, 5-8% click rate
- Email 5 (Community): 22-28% open rate, 4-6% click rate
