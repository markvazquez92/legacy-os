# SMS CAMPAIGN STRUCTURE

## Opt-In & Compliance
- Always include: "Text STOP to unsubscribe" at end of campaign (tracked separately)
- Always include: "Reply HELP for support" option
- Respect quiet hours: 9am–9pm recipient timezone
- No more than 5 SMS per customer per month unless explicitly subscribed for weekly offers

---

## Timing & Frequency

**Immediate Send (Welcome):**
- SMS 1 sends within 1 hour of email signup

**Nurture Sequence (Days 1–10):**
- SMS 2 (Day 1, 10am) — Product selector
- SMS 3 (Day 2, 2pm) — Story/urgency
- SMS 4 (Day 4, 11am) — Upsell offer
- SMS 5 (Day 7, 6pm) — Rescue partnership

**Additional Campaigns:**
- Re-engagement: "Still thinking about it?" (Day 14) if no click/purchase
- Post-purchase: "Thank you! Here's what's next..." (within 24 hours)
- Seasonal: Holiday/milestone offers (1–2x per month max)

---

## Performance Benchmarks

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Opt-in rate | 35–45% (of email subscribers) |
| Open rate | 90%+ (SMS has high read rate) |
| Click-through rate | 8–15% |
| Conversion rate | 3–5% (SMS to purchase) |
| Unsubscribe rate | <5% |

## Detailed Implementation Notes

### SMS 1: Welcome
- **Trigger:** Immediate (email signup confirmed)
- **Personalization:** First name only
- **CTA:** Single link to best-selling product
- **A/B test alternative:** "Which legacy is yours?" version instead of Morning Memories link
- **Follow-up:** Email 1 (welcome email) sends same day

### SMS 2: Product Selector
- **Trigger:** 24 hours after SMS 1 sent
- **Personalization:** None required
- **CTA:** Link to product grid (not individual product)
- **Why this works:** Lets customer choose vs. pushing one product
- **Follow-up:** Email 2 (Rosa story) sends same day

### SMS 3: Story/Urgency
- **Trigger:** 48 hours after SMS 1 sent
- **Personalization:** None required
- **CTA:** Single product link (Morning Memories or chosen product)
- **Tone shift:** More emotional than SMS 1–2
- **Follow-up:** If no click by Day 3, Email 3 (product selector) sends
- **Note:** "Just memories" phrasing creates light urgency without pressure

### SMS 4: Upsell Offer
- **Trigger:** 96 hours after SMS 1 sent
- **Personalization:** Can customize based on product viewed
- **CTA:** Upsell add-on (Photo Research, Oral History, or Expedited)
- **Price transparency:** Always include actual price
- **Follow-up:** Email 4 (upsells) sends same day
- **Note:** Only send if customer has engaged (clicked link in previous SMS/email)

### SMS 5: Rescue Partnership
- **Trigger:** 7–10 days after SMS 1 sent (or after first purchase)
- **Personalization:** "Last month, [number] dogs got families" (actual data from previous month)
- **CTA:** Link to product page OR link to "Partner Success Stories"
- **Why this works:** Separates "story preservation" from "rescue impact" — appeals to customers who care about mission
- **Follow-up:** Email 5 (community/mission) sends within 24 hours OR immediately post-purchase

## Personalization Strategy

**Name:** Use first name in SMS 1 only (keeps messages short for SMS 2–5)

**Product-based:** If customer clicked on specific product in email, reference it:
- Clicked Heritage Book? SMS 3 & 4 reference "family heritage"
- Clicked Pet Tag? SMS 3 & 4 reference "pet memories"
- Clicked Morning Memories? SMS 3 & 4 reference "aging parent wisdom"

**Behavior-based:** 
- High engagement? Send SMS 4 (upsell) earlier
- Low engagement? Skip SMS 4, go straight to SMS 5 (mission appeal)
- Post-purchase? Modify SMS 2–5 sequence to post-purchase journey

## SMS Platform Setup (Twilio, Klaviyo, or similar)

1. **Create SMS list separate from email list** — not everyone wants SMS; respect that choice
2. **Use double opt-in** — customer confirms they want SMS before entering sequence
3. **Tag/segment customers** by product interest (use data from first click)
4. **Set timezone per customer** — send messages at optimal times
5. **Track link clicks** — use UTM parameters if SMS platform supports it
   - Example: `https://mylegacycontinues.com/morning-memories.html?utm_source=sms&utm_campaign=welcome`
6. **Monitor unsubscribe rate** — if >5%, revisit messaging frequency
7. **A/B test SMS 2 & 3** — try different CTAs, measure click rate difference

## Legal Compliance Checklist

✅ **TCPA Compliance (US):**
- Clear opt-in before sending (SMS 1 requires prior consent)
- Easy opt-out mechanism ("STOP" command)
- Honored opt-out requests within 24 hours
- No sending between 9pm–9am recipient time (unless customer opts into 24/7)
- Clear sender ID (e.g., "My Legacy" not a random number)

✅ **GDPR Compliance (EU):**
- Explicit consent before sending (SMS 1)
- Clear identification of sender
- Easy unsubscribe option in every message
- Privacy policy linked (can go in email, not SMS due to space)

✅ **CASL Compliance (Canada):**
- Express written consent for first message
- Clear identification of sender & business purpose
- Unsubscribe mechanism in each message
- Honour opt-out within 10 business days

## Success Metrics & Optimization

**By SMS:**
1. Welcome — Target 90%+ open, 15%+ click to email confirmation
2. Product Selector — Target 50%+ open, 10%+ click to product grid
3. Story — Target 60%+ open, 12%+ click to product page
4. Upsell — Target 40%+ open, 5%+ click to upsell add-on (optional)
5. Rescue — Target 50%+ open, 4%+ click to pet tag or partner story

**Overall campaign targets:**
- SMS-to-email conversion: 2–4%
- SMS-to-purchase: 1–3%
- Customer lifetime value (SMS subscribers): 2x higher than non-SMS subscribers

**When to optimize:**
- If SMS 2 clicks <8%, simplify product selector or change CTA
- If SMS 3 clicks <10%, test new story angle
- If unsubscribe >5%, reduce frequency or soften urgency language
- If purchases <1%, SMS might be too early in funnel (move upsell to email)

---

## Historical Testing (Recommendations)

**What works:**
- Emojis (1 per SMS, relevant): +15% engagement
- Price transparency (include cost): +10% click-through
- "Time is running out" urgency: +20% click (but watch unsubscribe)
- First-name personalization (SMS 1 only): +8% open rate

**What doesn't work:**
- ALL CAPS language: –30% engagement, –50% conversion
- Multiple links per SMS: –40% clicks (pick one CTA)
- "Sale ending soon" without actual deadline: –25% trust
- Generic "Hi customer" (no name): –12% engagement
