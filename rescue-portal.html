What Every Rescue Partner Gets

Free Dog Profiles

Every dog in your care gets a permanent profile page — photos, personality, medical info, adoption link. We build it. You just submit the info.

Free — Always

QR Tag Transfer

When a dog gets adopted, the profile stays live. New owner gets a QR tag for the collar — one scan brings them home if they ever get lost.

Tag at cost to adopter

Transport Coordination

Free — Always

Revenue Share

A portion of every pet tag sale goes directly to our rescue partners. No middleman. No platform fees. Your dogs generate support just by being listed.

Direct to you

Sponsor a Dog

Supporters can sponsor a specific dog's medical care, transport, or training. Full transparency. Their name goes on the profile as a guardian.

Coming Soon

Adoption Follow-Up

Automated check-ins at 2 weeks, 1 month, 3 months. New owners submit updates and photos. You stay connected to every dog you placed.

Free — Always

When the Lake Hughes fire hit, animals were displaced with no coordination system. No profiles. No tracking. No way to reunite families with their pets. We built My Legacy Continues so that never happens again. Every rescue dog gets a permanent record. Every transport gets tracked. Every adoption gets followed up. This is the system that should have existed years ago — and we're giving it to rescues for free.
