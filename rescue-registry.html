Rescue Stories

Every dog deserves a permanent record.

Free, forever-hosted profiles for rescue dogs. In partnership with Underdog Heroes, Lompoc CA.

How It Works

Behavioral Journey

Rescuers and fosters document behavior, temperament, and progress. A complete picture before adoption.

Foster Updates

Real-time updates from foster families. Photos, milestones, medical records. All in one permanent profile.

Forever Profile

After adoption, the profile transfers to the new owner. Updates continue forever. When the dog passes, it becomes a memorial.

Our Rescue Dogs

Join The Rescue Network

We follow the animal from rescue to forever home to final rest.

Red List Alert Network

Coming Soon: Real-time euthanasia notifications sent directly to rescues and fosters.

Inspired by Bowie's Law and the work of shelter reform advocates. When a dog is placed on a euthanasia list, nearby rescues and qualified fosters get an immediate alert. No more missed saves.

How Your Purchase Supports Rescue

10% of Tags to Rescue

Every pet tag sale sends 10% directly to Underdog Heroes. Your purchase funds food, medical care, and transport for rescue dogs.

Heritage Books Fund Rescue Families

When rescue families purchase heritage books, part of that goes right back into the rescue ecosystem. Community funding community.

Business Profiles Empower Coordinators

Rescue coordinators get free business profiles to document their organizations. Permanent records, zero cost.

QR Codes = Accountability

Every QR profile creates a permanent, public record. Transparency in rescue. Every dog tracked from intake to forever home.
