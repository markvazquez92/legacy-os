3-3-3 Post-Adoption Check-In System

Adoption doesn't end at the handoff. We follow up.

Day 2 & Day 10

Initial check-ins to make sure the dog is adjusting and the family has support. We catch problems before they become returns.

Week 3 & Month 1.5

Behavioral follow-up. Is the dog settling? Does the family need training resources? We connect them with what they need.

Month 3

Final milestone check. By now the dog is home. The profile updates to reflect their new life. The record is permanent.

Behavioral Journey Tracker

Every dog's behavior is documented from intake through foster to adoption. Adopters know exactly who they're getting.

Intake Assessment

Temperament, reactivity, socialization level, medical condition. The full picture from day one. No surprises, no guessing.

Foster Progress Notes

Foster families log daily observations. Leash behavior, food aggression, house training, interaction with kids and other animals. All recorded.

Training Milestones

Every command learned, every behavior corrected, every breakthrough documented. The adopter inherits a complete behavioral history.

Adoption-Ready Profile

When a dog is ready, their profile tells the whole story. No more adopted dogs returned because the rescue oversold them. Transparency wins.

Rescue Accountability Ledger

Every dog logged publicly. Intake, condition, status, outcome. Prevents abuse from hiding behind a nonprofit ID.

Public Intake Records

Every dog that enters rescue is logged with date, source, condition, and photos. No dog disappears from the record.

Status Tracking

In foster, in medical, available, adopted, deceased. Every status change is timestamped and public. Accountability is automatic.

Outcome Reporting

Where did each dog end up? Adopted, transferred, or lost. The ledger tells the truth. No rescue can hide a bad track record behind good marketing.
