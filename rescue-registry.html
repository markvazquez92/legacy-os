How It Works

Behavioral Journey

Rescuers and fosters document behavior, temperament, and progress. A complete picture before adoption.

Foster Updates

Real-time updates from foster families. Photos, milestones, medical records. All in one permanent profile.

Forever Profile

After adoption, the profile transfers to the new owner. Updates continue forever. When the dog passes, it becomes a memorial.

The 3-3-3 Post-Adoption Check-In System

Adoption is not the finish line. Every adopter in our network gets automatic check-ins at Day 2, Day 10, Week 3, Month 1.5, and Month 3. Personal support through every transition stage. No other rescue platform does this.

Behavioral Journey Tracker

Every dog has a documented behavioral journey. From intake condition through foster observations to adoption day. You know exactly who you are getting. No surprises. No returns. Just a real match.

Rescue Accountability Ledger

Every dog that enters our network is logged publicly. Name. Intake date. Condition on arrival. Current status. Outcome. Transparency is not optional. Every life we touch is documented. This is how we prevent what happened with Rock N Pawz — 316 animals on a property zoned for four, three years of ignored complaints.
