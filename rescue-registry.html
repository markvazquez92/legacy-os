Who's Behind This

The People Doing the Work

Underdog Heroes is out of Lake Hughes, California. They're the ones in the trenches — coordinating transports for high-risk animals across the LA and Santa Barbara County shelter systems. They're the reason dogs like Bear and Duke ever had a shot at getting out.

Santa Barbara County Animal Services (SBCAS) is where Duke is right now. They receive, vet, and rehome the animals that come through. Every dog that lands there deserves a permanent profile — because when the shelter clears its records, this page doesn't.

My Legacy Continues handles the digital side — the profiles, the QR tags, the permanent hosting, and the funding connection. When you buy anything on this site, a piece of it keeps this network running. That's not a slogan. That's how the math works.
