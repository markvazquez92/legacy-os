The Guardian Protocol

How Every Dog Gets a Permanent Profile

01
Dog Enters the Rescue

When Underdog Heroes takes in a dog, My Legacy Continues builds them a free permanent profile — photos, personality, medical info, adoption link.

02
Profile Goes Live

Hosted at mylegacycontinues.com. Shareable. No login to view. Anyone can scan or click to see the full record.

03
Dog Gets Adopted

The profile stays live — updated with their new family. This is a legacy, not a listing. The story doesn't end at adoption.

04
Tag Transferred to Owner

The new owner gets a QR tag for the collar. Same profile, now with their contact info. One scan brings the dog home if they get lost.

Partnership

Underdog Heroes

A Riverside, CA nonprofit rescuing abused, injured, and neglected dogs from high-kill shelters. Founded by Shoshi Gamliel — they provide medical care, rehabilitation, and training before placing dogs in permanent homes.

My Legacy Continues provides every dog in their care a free permanent profile. A portion of every pet tag sale supports their operations directly.
