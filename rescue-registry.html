What Makes This Different

More Than a Listing

Permanent Profile
Every dog gets a permanent page that never expires. Petfinder removes listings after adoption. We keep their story alive forever.
Transfers to Adopter
When adopted, the profile becomes theirs. QR tag links here. Medical info, personality, their whole story — in the adopter's hands.
Real Stories
Not a data form. Each dog gets a written narrative — who they are, what they've been through, and what they deserve.
QR Tag Ready
Every adopted dog can get a My Legacy Continues QR tag linked to their profile. If they get lost, their finder sees everything.
