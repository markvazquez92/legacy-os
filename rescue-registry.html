ForeverProfile Stays Live

This isn't Petfinder. Here's why it's different.

Petfinder pulls the listing the day a dog gets adopted. We keep it alive — and hand it to the family. That's not a small difference. It's a completely different idea of what a pet record should be.

Profile Transfers at Adoption
When a dog finds their home, we hand the profile to the adopter. Medical history, personality, their whole rescue story — it all goes with them.
QR Tag Becomes Their ID
The QR tag on their collar links to this profile forever. If they ever get lost, whoever finds them scans it and knows exactly how to get them home.
Real Stories, Not Data Forms
Each dog gets a written profile — who they are, what they've been through, what kind of home they'd thrive in. Not checkboxes. A real story.
No Expiration Date
The profile lives permanently at mylegacycontinues.com. Whether they're still looking or already home, their record stays live. No subscription needed.
