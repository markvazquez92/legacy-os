Privacy Policy

How we protect your family's legacy.

Your Privacy Matters

At My Legacy Continues, we believe your family's stories and memories are sacred. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and respect your data.

What Information We Collect

When you use My Legacy Continues, we may collect:

  • Your name, email address, and phone number
  • Family member names, stories, and biographical information
  • Photos and documents you upload
  • Information about your preferences and how you use our service
  • Technical information (browser type, IP address, pages visited)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and maintain your family's legacy profiles and records
  • Process payments and fulfill orders
  • Communicate with you about your account and our services
  • Improve and personalize your experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

We never sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Data Security & Encryption

We take data security seriously. All information transmitted to My Legacy Continues is encrypted in transit using industry-standard HTTPS encryption. Your family profiles can be password-protected (optional) and are stored securely on encrypted servers.

We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of personal information.

Data Retention & Permanence

Your family's legacy data is hosted permanently. Once created, your profiles remain in our care indefinitely—protected, secure, and under your control. You may request deletion of your account at any time, but we recommend preserving your family's legacy for future generations.

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, remember your preferences, and understand how you interact with our site. You can control cookie settings through your browser, though some features may not work properly if cookies are disabled.

Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services for:

  • Payment processing (Stripe)
  • Form submissions (Formspree)
  • Analytics and improvement

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

Your Rights & Control

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Update or correct your information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications
  • Control privacy settings on family profiles

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

Children's Privacy

My Legacy Continues is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Parents or guardians who believe we have collected information about a child should contact us immediately.

California Residents

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). You have the right to know, delete, and opt-out of the sale of your personal information (which we do not do). For details, contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or by posting a notice on our website. Your continued use of My Legacy Continues after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
