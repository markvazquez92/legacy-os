The Story Behind It

Founder Story

Built from the Ground Up. Literally.

Bear Vazquez didn't learn this trade in a classroom. He learned it by watching his grandfather Joe — a metal craftsman from Mexico City who understood that the way you build something is the way it lasts.

Grandpa Joe made rings by hand. Bear pours foundations. Different scale, same standard.

McNeal Construction runs the same way it always has — no middleman, no inflated quotes, no subbing out the work and pocketing the difference.

You call Bear, Bear shows up. That's the whole operation.

Central Coast born. Central Coast built. This is home — and the work shows it.

The Heritage Behind the Business

My Legacy Continues — Vazquez Heritage Book

Every business has roots.
We documented ours.

The Vazquez name traces back to Galicia, Spain — 1547. From there to Mexico City to the Central Coast of California. Bear's grandfather was a master craftsman. That lineage lives in every job Bears Concrete takes on. My Legacy Continues documented the full story — the research, the migration, the trade tradition — in a printed Heritage Book and permanent digital profile.
