The Founder

Nick McNeal

Founder & General Contractor

Nick McNeal didn't start McNeal Construction because it was the easy path. He started it because he saw a gap: clients who deserved better workmanship, cleaner communication, and a contractor who actually showed up.

Based in Santa Barbara County, McNeal Construction operates across the Central Coast with a philosophy rooted in doing the job right the first time. No shortcuts. No runaround. Just skilled work and a contractor you can trust with your property.

Every project — residential or commercial — carries the same standard. The license number on the side of his truck is the same one you can look up today on the CSLB database. That accountability is built in.
